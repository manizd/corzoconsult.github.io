Doctor-Dentist Programs

We have a unique specialty in helping Dentist, Dermatologist, and Doctors in practice to strategize with certainty a succinct method to pay off financing debt and assist them in setting up funding to use in practice that they control. Building assets for these professions is a number one priority. The profession itself comes with risk, so we show you how to build safe growing assets to use throughout your practice years and through retirement. Retirement guarantees planned early in your career create less stress and avoids the open-ended practice option of what do I do when I want to sell or retire. How do I treat my practice, when I call it quits? Our analysis can assist you in seeing your way through. We use techniques/strategies from the book “Rich Dentist, Poor Dentist” by Rich Clouse.

At LaBroi Insurance Group, our #1 goal is to assist you in building assets through “LIVING BENEFITS” insurance products, while eliminating debts and creating a by-product of Generational Wealth!
